PB & J

Description

What a classic. Get ready to enjoy this fantastic sandwich. Nothing like consuming copious amounts of peanut butter that gets stuck to the roof of your mouth. Milk will be needed.

If you want to get extra crazy, use different jams and jellies for different flavors!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Natural peanut butter
  • Grape jelly/Jelly of choice

Steps

  1. Put peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Add equal amount of jelly on top
  3. Smack them together
  4. OPTIONAL: Toast it in a pan ;)
