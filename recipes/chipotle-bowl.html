Chipotle Bowl

Description

This recipe is an absolute banger. No longer do you have to go to Chipotle to eat like a king. This recipe is great for meal prep!

If you need to increase the number of servings in the recipe, it is very easy to just double all of the ingredients. Most everything is made seperately anyway, so it is pretty easy to adjust.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Black beans (or Pinto beans)
  • Rice of choice
  • Lettuce
  • Corn
  • Avocado
  • Pico de gallo
  • White cheese
  • Nonfat plain greek yogurt (or Sour cream)

Steps

  1. Cook all ingredients seperately
  2. Grab a plate
  3. Add desired amounts of ingredients
  4. Top with yogurt/sourcream, avocado, and cheese
  5. Enjoy!
