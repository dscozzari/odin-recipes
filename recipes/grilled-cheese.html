Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a fan favorite. Easily eaten by itself or with the dynamic duo combo item: tomato soup.

I always run out of grilled cheese before I run out of soup so...make more than one ;)

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Mayo
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put mayo on both slices of bread
  2. Layer at least 2 slices of cheese and form the sandwich
  3. Heat a pan on med/high heat
  4. Drop a slice of butter in the pan and place the sandwich in it
  5. Cook each side until they get a nice toast and cheese melts
  6. Slice, and dip in soup
